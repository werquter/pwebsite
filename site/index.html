«History of creation»

This brand was created by two friends who faced the problem of non-standard clothing size for the European size grid. They understood that this problem is quite common for girls in Uzbekistan. The average population growth is significantly lower than in European countries. With this thought, two friends decided to create their own brand with an emphasis on the girls of Uzbekistan. With the help of special constructors, two friends created their own size grid, in which they will find their size for a girl whose parameters do not correspond to standard size grids.
